Privacy Policy

PokePrice · Last updated: April 28, 2026

PokePrice ("the app", "we", "us") is a Pokémon Trading Card Game price-tracking app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

1. Information We Collect

PokePrice does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. The app does not require an account, does not track you, and does not contain any analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to us:

  • Your search history
  • Your favorite cards
  • Recently viewed cards
  • Cached card data, prices, and exchange rates (for offline access and faster loading)
  • App preferences (language, currency)

You can clear this data at any time from within the app or by deleting and reinstalling PokePrice.

3. Third-Party Services

To show card information and prices, the app makes requests to public, third-party APIs. These services may log standard HTTP request metadata (IP address, user agent) under their own privacy policies:

  • Pokémon TCG API — card metadata and images
  • PriceCharting — market prices
  • PokéAPI — multi-language name translation
  • Open Exchange Rates API — currency conversion

PokePrice does not share any user-identifying information with these services. Only the search query or card identifier is sent.

4. Camera & Live Text

PokePrice does not access your camera, photos, or microphone. The app uses Apple's built-in Live Text feature, which is part of iOS and is governed by Apple's privacy policy. Any text recognition happens on your device by iOS itself, not by PokePrice.

5. Children's Privacy

PokePrice is suitable for general audiences, including children. Because the app collects no personal information from any user, it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU GDPR-K.

6. Data Security

All data the app stores is kept locally on your device using iOS standard secure storage. Network requests use HTTPS. We do not operate any servers that handle your data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by a new "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after a change means you accept the updated policy.

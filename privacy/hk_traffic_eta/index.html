Privacy Policy

HK Traffic Unified ETA · Last updated: May 2, 2026

HK Traffic Unified ETA ("the app", "we", "us") aggregates real-time bus arrival data for Hong Kong public transport. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

1. Information We Collect

HK Traffic Unified ETA does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. The app does not require an account, does not track you, and does not contain any analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to us:

  • Your favorite routes and stops
  • Cached route catalogue and stop metadata (refreshed weekly for offline access and faster loading)
  • App preferences (language)

You can clear this data at any time by deleting and reinstalling the app.

3. Third-Party Services

To show real-time bus arrivals, the app makes requests to public, government-published OpenData APIs operated by Hong Kong public transport companies. These services may log standard HTTP request metadata (IP address, user agent) under their own privacy policies:

  • Kowloon Motor Bus (KMB) ETA Open Data — KMB & Long Win Bus routes, stops, and arrivals
  • Citybus (CTB) ETA Open Data — Citybus routes, stops, and arrivals
  • Green Minibus (GMB) ETA Open Data — Green Minibus routes, stops, and arrivals

HK Traffic Unified ETA does not share any user-identifying information with these services. Only the route, stop, or operator identifier needed to fetch arrival data is sent.

4. External Map Apps

When you tap the map icon next to a bus stop, the app opens your device's installed maps application (Apple Maps on iOS, or another map app you have installed) at the stop's coordinates. The opened map application is governed by its own privacy policy — we do not control how it handles your location or queries. No personal data is included in the request; only the stop's latitude, longitude, and name.

5. Location Services, Camera & Microphone

The app does not access your location, camera, photos, microphone, or contacts. The app does not request any of these permissions.

6. Children's Privacy

HK Traffic Unified ETA is suitable for general audiences, including children. Because the app collects no personal information from any user, it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU GDPR-K.

7. Data Security

All data the app stores is kept locally on your device using iOS standard secure storage. Network requests use HTTPS. We do not operate any servers that handle your data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by a new "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after a change means you accept the updated policy.

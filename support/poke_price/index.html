Frequently Asked Questions

Where do prices come from?

Prices are sourced from the Pokémon TCG API, which aggregates current TCGPlayer market data. Values are estimates of the secondary-market price in USD and are converted to other currencies using daily exchange rates.

How often are prices updated?

Prices are refreshed automatically when you open a card, with cached values served first for speed and a background update applied if the cache is older than 24 hours.

Why can I not find a specific card?

The app searches the public card database. Some recently released or regional-only cards may not yet be indexed. Try searching by the card name only, or by card number.

Why is a price showing as $0 or missing?

For very rare cards, brand-new releases, or low-volume cards, market data may not yet be available. Prices return as soon as enough sales data exists.

How do I clear my favorites or search history?

Open the Favorites tab and swipe a card to delete it. Search history and recently viewed cards can be cleared from the Home screen using the “Clear” button next to each section.

Is my data shared?

No. The app does not collect or transmit any personal information. All your data (favorites, search history) stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Does the app work offline?

Cards you have already viewed are cached and viewable offline. New searches and price refreshes require an internet connection.
